Azerbaijani Cuisine

Azerbaijani cuisine is a unique blend of flavors, spices, and traditions that reflect the country’s rich cultural heritage. It is known for its aromatic rice dishes, delicious kebabs, and sweet desserts.

Popular Dishes

  • Plov: A fragrant rice dish with saffron, meat, and dried fruits.
  • Dolma: Grape leaves stuffed with minced meat and rice.
  • Lyulya Kebab: Grilled minced lamb skewers.
  • Piti: A traditional lamb stew cooked in clay pots.
  • Pakhlava: A sweet pastry made with nuts and honey.

Tea Culture

Tea holds a special place in Azerbaijani culture. It is often served with jam or sweets, symbolizing hospitality and friendship.
