History of Azerbaijan

Azerbaijan has a rich and ancient history, dating back thousands of years. It has been a crossroads for civilizations, connecting East and West through the Silk Road. The country's history is marked by the influence of Persian, Ottoman, and Russian empires, as well as its unique cultural identity.

Key Historical Events

  • Formation of the Caucasian Albania in antiquity.
  • Role of Azerbaijan in the Silk Road trade.
  • Independence movement and the establishment of the Azerbaijan Democratic Republic in 1918.
  • Modern independence in 1991 after the collapse of the Soviet Union.
