Tourism in Azerbaijan

Azerbaijan is a land of diverse landscapes, where ancient culture meets modern architecture. From the Caspian Sea to the majestic Caucasus Mountains, the country offers something for every traveler.

Top Tourist Attractions

  • Gobustan National Park - home to ancient rock carvings.
  • Flame Towers - iconic modern architecture in Baku.
  • Sheki Khan’s Palace - a masterpiece of Azerbaijani architecture.
  • Naftalan Spa - unique oil therapy experience.
  • Caspian Sea resorts - relax by the largest lake in the world.

Adventure Awaits

For nature enthusiasts, explore the lush forests of Ganja, trek in the Greater Caucasus, or visit the stunning mud volcanoes. Azerbaijan's tourism is as unique as its rich cultural heritage.
